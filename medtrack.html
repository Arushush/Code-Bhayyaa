About Med Track School

Med Track School assists school medical faculties in managing and maintaining the health records of students digitally. This platform simplifies the task of tracking student health information and enhances the ability to provide timely medical care.

Our Features

Annual Health Record

Store, track, and update each student's health history for comprehensive care. It helps track conditions like allergies, diseases, medications, and more.

  • Accurate student health records
  • Flagging of high-risk students
  • Data used for future updates

Medical Room Tracking

Monitor student visits to the medical room, log treatments, and record symptoms for improved follow-up care and analysis of recurring issues.

  • Track symptoms and treatment details
  • Identify students with recurring issues
  • Improved follow-up and analysis

Data Security & Accessibility

Student records are securely stored and easily accessible by authorized personnel, enabling quick medical action during emergencies.
